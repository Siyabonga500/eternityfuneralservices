Partnership with AGC

Creating Sustainable Income for Church Development

Eternity Funeral Services (Pty Ltd) is partnering with African Gospel Church (AGC) to create a sustainable source of income for the church while providing valuable financial protection to members.

The church currently relies solely on offerings to finance important services such as youth conventions, Sunday school training, and development projects. Our partnership will help the church meet its financial needs without reducing essential services.

How it works: We add 15% to each policy premium (e.g., R150 + 15%), and this additional amount is collected for the church. This creates a win-win situation where members receive quality funeral cover while supporting church development.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a member moves to another church group?

Eternity Funeral Services (Pty Ltd) is an independent entity and not dependent on the church or its owners. Your policy remains valid and secure regardless of your church affiliation changes. The partnership with AGC is for mutual benefit, but your coverage is with our company directly.

Where and how do I pay my premiums?

Premiums can be paid through multiple convenient channels:

  • Directly at our branch offices
  • Bank deposits (using your unique reference number)
  • Electronic funds transfer (EFT)
  • Debit order facilities (available on request)
What is the waiting period for claims?

We offer minimal waiting periods compared to industry standards:

  • Natural causes: 6 months waiting period
  • Accidental death: Covered from day 1
  • Extended family members: 12 months waiting period
How do I submit a claim?

Claims can be submitted 24/7 through our dedicated claims line. You'll need to provide:

  • Death certificate
  • Policyholder ID document
  • Completed claim form
  • Banking details for beneficiary

Our claims team will guide you through the entire process with compassion and efficiency.
